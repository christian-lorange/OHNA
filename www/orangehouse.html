About the App

We feel that noise is one of the most unrespected and misunderstood health risks in the work place. It is so easy to say "Its not that loud" or "I'm only going to be around this loud noise for a little bit of time." The reality is that just a small amount of time in a loud environment can damage your hearing for life. Once your hearing is damaged there is no going back. We also understand that the math associated with calculating noise exposure can be confusing and overwhelming.

Why didn't I pay more attention when they were teaching me log calculations and debicle scales? Aghh!

We developed this tool to help health and safety professionals conduct noise exposure assessments. You can quickly and conviently use the app to determine a workers time weighted exposure to noise and thereby better assist them in determining what corrective acctions might need to be taken or what PPE might be needed.
